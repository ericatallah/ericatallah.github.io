My Work Experience

With close to a decade of experience in web development, I've worked for enterprise level corporations such as 3M and Envestnet. I've consulted for large entertainment companies such as Disney, and real estate firms such as Xome, Long & Foster, and Berkshire Hathaway Home Services. I've worked on small dev teams and large dev teams, almost always in scrum environments.
